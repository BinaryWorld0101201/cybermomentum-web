CyberMomentum BETA

Let's analyze Information shared by CyberSecurity Community !

CyberSecurityMomentum wants to provide a "nerdy", premature, beta, incomplete and "early stage" dashboard in order to build a self-idea about cybersecurity information in a specific amount of time. CyberMomentum provides sentiment analysis, information impact, information "trustability" (in terms of verified accounts) and general flow analysis on cybersecurity information, it would be a useful tool for Security Researches and Security Journalist to map the CyberSecurity Momentum in order to better adress analyses and reportage.

This project is till in BETA release. Due to lack of resources everything is "slow" and "improvable". If you feel, please make a donation in order to improve this project.

Feature List

Feature List

Information Languages

Detected languages is often interesting to address the community and to understand where the information moves. For example if from Tick 3 the most important information are written on plain Italian language, it means the Italian community gets more and more interested on the topic. In this example we might observe an increment of interest on Italian community which could be either: more Italian professional are going to work on that topic or the information took place mainly in Italy or for some reasons it's an important information for such a society if compared to other society.

Sentiment Analysis

A graph bar shows the sentiment about the momentum on cybersecurity. Generally speaking people talk about cybersecurity during incidents and this has not a positive feelings, but understanding how much negative is or how the feeling about cybersecurity is evolving over time is not a trivial question to answer and quite interesting in order to make an own idea about the general feelings.

Information Distribution

How the analyzed information, in terms of quantity, evolves over time. If it increases the number of information about cybersecurity it could be a nice indicator about new coming threats, crime, of important events. On the other side if the number of cybersecurity information slowly decreases it could be an indicator of general disinterest on cybersecurity or the outgoing from hot topic to warm topic..

Information Confidence

If the information sources have many followers the information confidence would increase, contrary if the information sources have few followers it might be a low level confidence information. I am sure this is not a rocket science, but it could be a beginning of structured approch to evaluate Cyber Impact on those circumstances.

Information Impact

Bigger is the information source network, bigger would be the information impact over the society. Generally thinking much higher is the source network much higher would be it impact in terms of distribution. Indeed the society impact could be a funcitoin depending to time-frame, nationality and relationship. In fact a 20 follower source would have some difficulties to spread information if comparedto to one,

Trustability

If the information source has been validate it trustability degrees would increase a lot. Measuring information coming from general public or information coming from specific profiles would change the trustability of the information. Usually verified accounts are reflecting wellknown people on a gien topic and usually those people gave us and stimuli to learn and to build new things.

Top Information Sharing Users

Simply the top sharing accounts or users (Twitter only, so far). This information could be intereseting for grabbing informations about top contributor.

Tick Number

Tick represents observations. They are every hour or so (depending on computational time). The data is refreshed from time to time depending on DataBase size. This is not an issue at all since we are interested only over the current momentum and not to the past one. If you are interested on data before DB resetting (which will happen) please referr to the Momentum History Data (following).

MAP

Finally a derived Map, based on information localisation. This information is grouped by city and derived from user nationality which is indipendent from the current user GPS location. So for example if Bob writes to Alice while he is on holiday in Miami, but Bob is from Paris the location would be with high probabilitiy located in Paris.
